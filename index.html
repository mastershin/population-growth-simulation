Population-growth-simulation

Uses birth rate & death rate from actual statistics to simulate population growth model. Used d3.js & Javascript library

Population Simulator

Jae Y Shin Email: mastershin@gmail.com LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/jaeyulshin

Main Algorithm: 1. Use birth rate and death rate to simulate population growth simulation. 2. 50% chance of male/female. 3. Redish Colors are female. Bluish colors are male.
